Programs For Campers

Day, Overnight, or ExplorAsian

Why Join Us?

Engage with Culture

Discover Chinese culture with hands-on activities such as Erhu, Guzheng, and calligraphy.

Learn Valuable Skills

Our programs emphasize teamwork, resilience, and leadership, preparing campers for the future.

Build Lifelong Friendships

Campers forge connections that go beyond camp, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Programs

Day Camp

Ages 6-10

Throughout the day, day campers travel in their groups to classes and activities, such as Chinese yo-yo, cooking, swimming, and arts and crafts. The week ends with a Closing Ceremony on Friday during which campers perform what they’ve learned. Lunch and snacks included.

Discover

Overnight Camp

Ages 10-14

During the day, campers travel in their groups to class and activities learning about Chinese culture and preparing for the end-of-the-week Closing Ceremony. At night, campers attend exciting evening activities, including the Talent Show, Carnival, CIT Dress-Up, and Movie Nights!

Explore

ExplorAsian

Ages 14-16

Established in 2002, ExplorAsian is usually a week-long, overnight program for students aged 14 to 16 who are interested in exploring Chinese-American identity and related issues, building leadership skills, and meeting new people. The goal is to empower youth to become leaders in their communities.

Day / Overnight Camp

Day camp runs from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Overnight campers stay in the dorms at Brandeis University from Sunday to Friday. Campers are divided into groups based on age and experience. Throughout the week, campers participate in a variety of activities, including Chinese yo-yo, Kung-fu, Calligraphy, and more. The week ends with a Closing Ceremony on Friday during which campers perform what they’ve learned. Watch the video to learn more about our programs and activities!
